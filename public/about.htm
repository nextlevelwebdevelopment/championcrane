About Champion Crane

Champion Crane Rental, Inc. one of the foremost crane rental companies in Southern California. Owned and operated by Mike Konle since 1983, Champion Crane has firmly established itself as an industry leader. Champion has assembled an impressive fleet of state-of- the-art hydraulic cranes ranging in size from 4 ton to 250 ton capacities with boom lengths of up to 350 feet, and specialty cranes like the popular 33 ton demag city crane available for rental only through Champion Crane. All equipment is fully maintained, operated, insured and certified.

In addition to cranes, Champion offers trucks, trailers, and a wide variety of accessories such as camera baskets, man baskets, spreader bars, concrete buckets, clamshells, breaking balls, rigging, and machinery moving equipment. With a full machine shop on site, Champion is equipped to fabricate custom lifting accessories. Our facility is available for pre-job test lifts or as a location for production companies.

Champion Crane has vast experience in all aspects of crane rental and specializes in a variety of areas such as motion picture and production work, special effects, stunt work, construction, steel erection, landscaping, air conditioning equipment, theme park attractions, aerospace, and machinery moving to name just a few.

Centrally located in San Fernando Valley, Champion Crane's service area includes, Los Angeles County, Ventura County, Orange County, Palmdale and Lancaster. Special projects regularly take us far North as Central California and South to San Diego.
